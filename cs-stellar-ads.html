Stellar Ads

Product Designer & Founder • 2024 - Present

Overview

Stellar Ads is an advanced advertising platform that leverages AI-powered targeting and real-time optimization to maximize campaign performance. As both Product Designer and Founder, I led the entire product development process from concept to launch.

Role

Duration

Team Size

Research & Discovery

I conducted extensive user research to understand the pain points in current advertising platforms. Through interviews with 15+ marketers and agency professionals, I identified key opportunities for improvement.

Key Insights

  • Existing platforms are overly complex and time-consuming to use
  • Campaign optimization requires constant manual intervention
  • Lack of real-time insights leads to missed opportunities
  • AI-powered features are often gimmicky rather than genuinely helpful

Design Process

I followed a user-centered design approach, iterating rapidly based on user feedback and data insights.

Key Features Designed

  • Intuitive campaign creation wizard
  • Real-time performance dashboard
  • AI-powered audience targeting
  • Automated optimization recommendations
  • Comprehensive analytics and reporting

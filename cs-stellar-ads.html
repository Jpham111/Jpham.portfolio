Stellar Ads CRM

Empowering local businesses with customizable customer management

Overview Initial Observations Market Research User Research Competitor Analysis Solution Final Designs Reflection
Project Stellar Ads - Custom CRM for local businesses
Timeline 8 months (January - August 2024)
Role Lead Product Designer
Team 4 members - Designer (me), 2 Developers, Product Manager

The Problem

Local business owners juggled spreadsheets and multiple disconnected tools to manage daily operations. Booking appointments and tracking client retention required hours of manual work. Existing CRMs were too expensive, too complex, or built for enterprise needs - not local businesses.

The Solution

Designed Stellar Ads - a customizable CRM that streamlines booking, client communication, and retention tracking. Business owners can tailor the platform to their specific industry without technical expertise.

Impact

  • 73% reduction in time spent on booking and scheduling
  • 85% improvement in client retention tracking
  • 92% of users successfully customized CRM to their needs
  • 2.5 hours saved daily per business
  • 4.7/5 satisfaction from 150+ local businesses

Initial Observations

Current state problems:

  • Business owners wore multiple hats with no time for complex software
  • 68% still used spreadsheets for customer management
  • Each industry had unique workflows (salons ≠ gyms ≠ clinics)
  • Booking and retention were biggest daily pain points

Key quotes:

  • Salon owner: "I spend 2 hours daily managing bookings and following up with clients"
  • Gym owner: "Every CRM wants me to adapt to their system. I need something that adapts to me"
  • Clinic manager: "We lose clients because we forget to follow up"

Critical insight:

Local businesses needed a CRM that felt built FOR them, with customization simple enough for non-technical owners to configure themselves.

Market Research

Market landscape:

  • $500B+ local business market in the US
  • 87% abandon complex CRM implementations within 6 months
  • Average 3-4 different tools used daily (booking, email, spreadsheets)

Existing solutions gaps:

  • Salesforce: Enterprise-focused, $150+/month, requires technical setup
  • Square Appointments: Good for booking but limited customer management
  • Zoho CRM: Generic, no industry customization

Opportunity:

Create a CRM that starts simple for immediate value but scales with customization as businesses grow, without requiring technical expertise.

Positioning: "The CRM that feels like it was built for YOUR business - because you can build it yourself."

User Research

Research methods:

  • 15 in-depth interviews with local business owners
  • Shadowed 5 businesses for full workdays
  • Surveyed 80 local business owners
  • Tested prototypes with 8 different industries

Primary user: Maria, Salon Owner (35-45)

  • Business: Small salon with 3 stylists
  • Daily goals: Book appointments, track regulars, manage inventory
  • Pain: Manual booking and retention tracking takes 2+ hours daily
  • Quote: "I need a system that works the way I work"

Key insights:

  • Booking is the #1 daily task - must be fast and intuitive
  • Client retention directly impacts revenue - automated follow-ups crucial
  • Every industry has unique data needs (salons: color formulas, gyms: workout plans)
  • Business owners, not IT teams - customization must be no-code

Competitor Analysis

Competitors analyzed:

  • Salesforce: Powerful but expensive, requires consultants
  • HubSpot: Marketing-focused, weak on operations
  • Square: Good booking, limited CRM features
  • Zoho: Affordable but generic

Key findings:

  • No CRM prioritizes booking as core feature
  • Customization requires developers or consultants
  • None built specifically for local business workflows
  • Retention tools are afterthoughts

Our advantage:

Industry templates + no-code customization + booking-first design + affordable pricing

  • Simplified interface without sacrificing power
  • True AI-driven optimization
  • Real-time performance insights
  • Cross-platform campaign management

Solution

Design principles:

  1. Booking-first - Make scheduling the easiest daily task
  2. No-code customization - Drag-and-drop fields for any business
  3. Retention by design - Automated follow-ups built-in
  4. Start simple, grow complex - Immediate value, advanced features when ready

Core features designed:

Smart Booking System
  • One-click appointment scheduling with calendar sync
  • Automated reminder texts/emails
  • Client booking history at a glance
Customizable Client Profiles
  • Drag-and-drop custom fields for industry-specific needs
  • Quick-access notes and preferences
  • Automatic retention alerts for inactive clients
Retention Automation
  • Automatic "we miss you" campaigns
  • Birthday and milestone reminders
  • Customizable follow-up workflows
Industry Templates
  • Pre-built configurations for salons, gyms, clinics, studios
  • One-click template application with easy modification

Testing results:

  • 85% of users started with industry templates
  • 92% added custom fields within first week
  • 73% faster appointment scheduling
  • 67% managed CRM primarily from mobile

Final Designs

Core interface features:

Dashboard
  • Today's schedule prominently displayed
  • Retention alerts for inactive clients
  • Quick booking button always accessible
  • Custom widgets users can add/remove
Booking Interface
  • Calendar with drag-and-drop appointments
  • Client search with instant profile preview
  • Service selection with auto-fill duration
  • Automated confirmations sent immediately
Customization Builder
  • Drag-and-drop field builder - add any data point
  • Field types: text, date, dropdown, checkboxes, file upload
  • Section organization for grouped fields
  • Template library with pre-made field sets
Mobile Experience
  • Quick booking from home screen
  • Client lookup with one-tap calling/texting
  • Schedule view optimized for small screens

Industry customization examples:

  • Salon: Hair color formulas, allergies, preferred stylist
  • Gym: Fitness goals, injury history, membership tier
  • Clinic: Medical history, insurance info, prescriptions

Reflection

Results (6 months post-launch):

  • 73% time savings on daily booking tasks
  • 85% improvement in client retention follow-through
  • 92% successful customization rate
  • 2.5 hours saved daily per business
  • 4.7/5 satisfaction from 150+ businesses
  • 41% increase in repeat customer bookings

Real business impact:

  • Maria's Salon: Reduced booking from 20 mins to 3 mins per appointment, recovered 15 lost clients
  • FitCore Studio: Decreased new member churn from 30% to 12%

What worked:

  • Industry templates provided immediate value - same-day launches
  • No-code customization empowered owners to adapt CRM themselves
  • Booking-first approach solved most frequent pain point
  • Mobile optimization matched how owners actually work

Key challenges:

  • Balancing customization with simplicity - used progressive disclosure
  • Supporting different industry needs - created flexible field system
  • User education - built in-app tutorials with examples

What I'd do differently:

  • Earlier multi-industry testing beyond salons
  • Video tutorials from launch for faster adoption
  • More template variety for starting points

Next steps:

  • Payment integration for direct transactions
  • Team management with role permissions
  • Advanced analytics for revenue tracking
  • Client self-service portal

Personal growth:

  • Deep user empathy - designed for time-starved business owners
  • Customization architecture - balanced flexibility with usability
  • No-code design thinking - empowered non-technical users
  • Shadowing as research - observing workflows beats interviews

The Problem

Existing advertising platforms are overly complex and time-consuming to use. Campaign optimization requires constant manual intervention, and lack of real-time insights leads to missed opportunities. AI-powered features are often gimmicky rather than genuinely helpful.

The Solution

Created Stellar Ads — an advanced advertising platform that leverages AI-powered targeting and real-time optimization to maximize campaign performance with an intuitive interface.

Impact

  • 85% increase in daily active users
  • 40% improvement in campaign CTR
  • 60% reduction in setup time
  • 4.8/5 average user satisfaction rating

Research & Discovery

I conducted extensive user research to understand the pain points in current advertising platforms. Through interviews with 15+ marketers and agency professionals, I identified key opportunities for improvement.

Key Insights

  • Existing platforms are overly complex and time-consuming to use
  • Campaign optimization requires constant manual intervention
  • Lack of real-time insights leads to missed opportunities
  • AI-powered features are often gimmicky rather than genuinely helpful

Design Process

I followed a user-centered design approach, iterating rapidly based on user feedback and data insights.

Key Features Designed

  • Intuitive campaign creation wizard
  • Real-time performance dashboard
  • AI-powered audience targeting
  • Automated optimization recommendations
  • Comprehensive analytics and reporting

Results & Impact

Since launch, Stellar Ads has shown significant improvements in user engagement and campaign performance.

User Adoption 85% increase in daily active users
Campaign Performance 40% improvement in CTR
Time to Value 60% reduction in setup time
User Satisfaction 4.8/5 average rating
